* Title

Spaghetti: Clustering-Aided Tractography Segmentation


* Authors (random order)

Paolo Avesani
Thien Bao Nguyen
Emanuele Olivetti
Eleftherios Garyfallidis
Nivedita Agarwal


* Abstract

In this work we present Spaghetti, a novel 3D interactive software
system to support neuroanatomists and medical doctors in exploring and
segmenting the white matter of the brain reconstructed by diffusion
magnetic resonance imaging (dMRI) techniques.
